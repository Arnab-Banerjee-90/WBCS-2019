Summary of 2011 Census
  • Fifteenth Census
  • Seventh census of Independent India
  • Slogan of 2011 Census "Our Census Our Future"
  • Total Population 121cr, Male 62.3cr (51.54%) and Female 58.75cr (48.46%)
  • In the duration of 2001-2011 Indian population has increased by 18cr (17.72%)
  • Male poulation increased by 17.19%, and female by 18.12%
  • Population density 382/sqkm
  • Female, male ratio 943:1000
  • Total literate people count 77.8cr (74.04%)
  • Male literace Rate 82.14%, and Female literacy rate 65.46%
  • Hindu Polulation is 80.5%
  • Himachal Pradesh has the hishest percentage of Hindu population 95.4%
  • Lakshwadweep has the highest Muslim percentage of 95.5%, followed by Jammu Kashmir 67%
  • Nagaland has the highest percentage of Christians at 90%
  • Sikkim has the highest percentage of Buddhists at 28%
