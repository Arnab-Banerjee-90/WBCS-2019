Energy Resources of India

Thermal Energy

Introduction
  • Thermal energy produces 67% of the worlds energy
  • China produces the most thermal energy (22.6%), US is second (19.8%) and India is third (5.3%)
  • Thermal energy produces 67% of India's power
  • NTPC established in 1975, to set up large thermal power plants across India
  • India has 16 Coal based and 7 gas based very large thermal power plants
  • As of 2012 NTPC produces 27% of India's total energy

Atomic Energy

Introduction
  • FIrst Atomic energy center in Tarapur Maharashtra in 1969
  • India is 11th in terms of atomic energy production
  • Atomic energy contributes to 4% of Total energy requirement
  • Nuclear Arc → Almost all nuclear plants in India are located in an arc formation in the south and western states near the ocean
  • At present there are 28 Nuclear Furnaces in India
  • In 1956 Apsara furnace was the first nuclear furnace of India
  • In 1960 Cyrus was the second furnace, main source for the POkhran atom bomb
