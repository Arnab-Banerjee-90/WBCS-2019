Creation of the States of India

Evolution Post 1956
Year Changes
1956 Delhi emerges as a Union Territory
1960 Gujarat and Maharashtra created from Bombay state
1962 Portuguese colonies such as Goa, Daman, Diu, Dadra Nagar Haveli, Pondicherry became Union Territories of India
1963 Nagaland given Statehood
1966
  • Punjab and Haryana created from Punjab state
  • Chandigarh emerged as a capital of the two states and as a Uinon territory
1969
  • Madras was renamed to Tamilnadu
  • Meghalaya emerged as a Union territory
1971
  • Manipur, Tripura Himachal Pradesh given Statehood
  • Mizoram emerged first as a Union Territory later as a state
1972 Meghalaya, Manipur and Tripura given Statehood
1973
  • Mysore was renamed to Karnataka
  • Lakshwadeep, Amindivi and Minicoy were renamed to Lakshwadeep
1975 Sikkim was integrated into India as a State
1986 North and South 24 Parganas came into existance by splitting 24 Parganas
1987
  • Arunachal Pradesh and Mizoram emerged as States
  • North and South 24 Parganas came into existance after splitting of 24 Parganas
1991 NCT act was passed and Delhi was given Statehood
1992
  • North and South Dinajpur came into existance by splitting of Paschim Dinajpur
  • Teen Bigha Corridor was leased to Bangladesh
