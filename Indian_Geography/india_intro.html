1950 to 1956
  • 1951 → Coochbihar was integrated into West Bengal
  • 1953 → Andhra Pradesh was formed by taking parts of Madras state. It was the first linguistic state of India, Patti Srimalaru was one of the most prominent activist in the demand for seperate state
  • 1954 → Part C states Himachal Pradesh and Bilaspur were merged to create a new state called Himachal Pradesh

Reorganization of States 1956
  • State Reorganization Commission created in 1953
  • As per the commission the states were reorganized on the basis of language
  • Kerala was formed in 1956 by merging Malabar district of Madras state with the native states of Travancore-Cochin
  • The ABCD categorization of the states was repealed
