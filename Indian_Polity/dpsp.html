Directive Principles Of State Policy

Nature Of the DPSPs
  • Part IV of the Constitution from Article 36 to Article 51
  • This novel feature was borrowed from the Constitution Of Ireland
  • This concept is the latest development in constitutional governments with growing acceptance of a Welfare State

DPSPs at a glance
Article Deals With
Article 36 Definition of State
Article 37 The Directive Principles are Non Justiciable but fundamental in nature that the State should apply these principles in making laws
Article 38 Directs state to secure and protect a social order that stands for the welfare of people
Article 39
  • Equal pay for equal work
  • Equitable distribution of resources
  • Preservation of health of workers and childern against abuse
  • Oppertunities for helathy development of childern
Article 40 Organize Village Panchayats
Article 41 Right to work, to education and to public assistance in case of unemployment, oldage, sickness and disability
Article 42 Provision of just and humane conditions of work and maternity relief
Article 43 To secure a living wage and decent standard of living. State shall endeavour to promote Cottage industries on an individual or co-operative basis in rural areas.
Article 43 A Participation of workers in management of induxtries
Article 43 B Promotion of cooperative societies
Article 44 Uniform civil code for all the citizens
Article 45 Provide free and compulsory education for for all children till the age of 14
Article 46 Promotion of educational and economic interest of the SCs, the STs and other weaker sections
Article 47 Raise the level of nutrition, bring about the prohibition of intoxicating drinks and drugs that are injurious to health
Article 48 Organization of agriculture and animal husbandry, prohibition of slaughter of cows, calvs and other milch and draught animals
Article 48 A Protection of environment and safegaurding forest and wildlife
Article 49 Protection of monuments and places and objects of national importance
Article 50 Seperation of judiciary from executive
Article 51 Promote international peace and security

Directives outside Part IV
