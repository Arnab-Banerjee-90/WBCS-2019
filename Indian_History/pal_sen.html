Pal and Sen Dynasties of Bengal

Matsanaya
  • Time period of 637-750 AD starting after the death of Shashanka
  • A time of political and social instability in Bengal
  • A Samantaraja named Gopala was nominated as the ruler of Bengal in 750 AD

The Palas

Gopala
  • Gopala was the first Pal ruler
  • He brought peace and stability back in Bengal
  • Succeeded by his son Dharmapala

Dharmapala
  • He was the Pal ruler involved in Tripartite struggle
  • Captured Kannauj
  • Nominated Chakrayudh as the ruler of Kannauj
  • Defeated by Gurjar king Nagabhatta II, who in turn was defeated by Rashtrakut ruler Govinda III
  • Dharmapala recaptured Kannauj after detah of Govinda III
  • His title was Parameshwar Parambhattrak Maharajadhiraj also called Uttarapathaswami
  • Succeeded by Devapala

Devapala
  • He was also involved in the Tripartite struggle
  • Pal empire reached its zenith under his rule
