Later Mughal Emperors

Bahadur Shah I (1707-12)
  • Bahadur Shah I defeated his brother Kam Baksh in the Battle of Jajau in 1707
  • He released Sahu the son of Sambhaji and recognised the independence of Mewar and Marwar
  • He was called Shah-i-Bekhabar
  • He made peace with Guru Govind Singh and allowed the Marathas to collect Sardeshmukhi of the Deccan
  • Led an unsuccessful campaign against Banda Bahadur, he died during the course of this campaign
  • A Dutch representative committee under the leadership of Jashua Ketelar visited his court in 1711

Jahandar Shah (1712-13)
  • Captured the throne by liquidating his brothers with the help of Zulfiqar Khan
  • Zulfikar Khan acted as the de-facto ruler
  • He was dominated by his mistress Lal Kunwar, who is said to have imitated the style of Nur Jahan
  • He granted the Marathas, the Chauth and Sardeshmukhi, but it had to be collected by a Mughal official
  • He was the first Mughal Emperor to be killed in captivity by the Sayyed Brothers

Farukhsiyar (1713-19)
  • Known as Sahid-i-Mazlum
  • He appointed Abdullah Khan as Wazir and Hussain Ali as Mir Bakshi
  • He appointed Chin Quilich Khan also known as Nizam-Ul-Mulk as the provincial governor of the Deccan
  • Nizam-ul-Mulk later laid the foundation of an independent state of Hyderabad in 1724
  • Sikh leader Banda-Bahadur was taken prisoned and later executed in 1716
  • He was executed by the Sayeed Brothers in 1719
  • Peshwa Balaji Vishwanath visited his court for the right to collect Chauth and Sardeshmukhi on Maratha land
  • Farukhsiyar gave the British East India Company a firman granting trade privileges

Mohammed Shah (1719-48)
  • He remained under the influence of Sayeed brothers during initial years of his reign
  • With the help of Chin Quilich Khan, he killed the Sayeed brothers in 1720
  • He was the most pleasure loving and was known as Rangeela
  • Marathas under Baji Rao I raided the surroundings of Delhi in 1737 for the first time
  • Nadir Shah of Persia invaded India, and with the help of Sadat Khan, defeated the Mughal army in the Battle of Karnal in 1739
  • The following rulers declared their independence
    • Sadat Ali Khan of Awadh
    • Murshid Kuli Khan of Bengal
    • Rohillas

Ahmed Shah (1748-54)
  • His reign saw the first invasion of Ahmed Shah Abdali, the general of Nadir Shah
  • Ahmed Shah Abdali invaded india five times during 1748-1767

Alamgir II (1754-59)
  • Also known as Aziz-Ud-din
  • Battle of Plassey in 1757 took place during his reign
  • He was killed by his wazir in 1759 and was replced by Shah Jahan II, who in turn got replced by Shah Alam II

Shah Alam II (1759-1806)
  • He was defeated in The battle of Buxar
  • Third Battle of Panipath was fought during his reign
  • He granted East India Company diwani rights of Bengal,Bihar and Orissa as per Treaty of Allahabad
  • In 1803 Lord Lake captured Delhi which was till then under Maratha control
  • Shah Alam II was the first Mughal emperor to become a pensioner of the East India Company

Akbar II (1806-37)
  • Lord Hastings asked him to give away all ceremonial titles and powers
  • Circulation of Mughal coins was stopped in 1835
  • He conferred the title of Raja on Ram Mohan Roy ans asked him to go to London to plead for a hike in Emperor's pension

Bahadur Shah II (1837-62)
  • He participted in the revolt of 1857
  • He was tried and executed to Rangoon in Burma
  • He died in 1862 in Rangoon
  • He patrnised Mirza Ghalib and gace him the Jagir of Loharu
