The Maratha Empire

Life of Shivaji
  • Born in Shivner in 1627
  • Shahji Bhonsle was his father, who was a military officer in the State of Ahmednangar
  • Sivaji was brought up in Poona under supervision of Dadaji Konadev
  • Also came under religious influence of Guru Samarth Ramdas
  • Captured Toorna fort in 1646, and built new forts like Raigarh and Pratapgarh
  • Coronated at Raigarh in 1674, and assumend the title of Haindava-dharma-dharak

Conquests of Shivaji
  • In 1656 conquered Javli from Maratha chief Chandra Rao More
  • Attacked the Adil-Shah kingdom of Bijapore in 1657-58, killed Afzal Khan in his own camp
  • Shaista Khan was appointed Governor of Deccan to check the rising power of Shivaji
  • In 1663 Shivaji attacked military camp of Shaista Khan, and plundered Surat in 1664
  • Aurangzeb sent his Prince Muazzam against Shivaji, which was a failure
  • Mirja Raja Jai Singh was sent thereafter, and he succeeded in in surrounding Shivaji in the fort of Puradhar
  • Treaty of Purandhar was signed in 1665
  • Shivaji renewed his war in the form of second plunder of Surat in 1670, and gradually regained many of his lost forts
  • Aurangzeb sent Diler Khan and Bahadur Khan against Shivaji, however Shivaji defeated them in the battle of Salher in 1672
  • He formed alliance with the Qutub Shahi rulers of Golconda and led a campaign into Bijapuri Karnataka, conquering Jingi and many other forts in Karnataka
  • Died in Raigarh in 1680

Successors Of Shivaji

Sambhaji
  • War of Succession was between Sambhaji the eldest son and Rajaram the youngest
  • Sambhaji ascended the throne in 1680
  • Placed his confidence in a Brahmin named Knahoji, whom he gave supreme control of the administration and the title of Kavi Kalash
  • Provided protection and support to Akbar II the rebellious son of Aurangzeb
  • In 1689 Sambhaji and Kavi Kalash were captured by Muqarrab Khan at Sangameshwar and put to death
  • Mughal army under Zulfiqar Khan launched an attack upon Raigarh, all the members of Sambhaji's family including his son Sahu were captured and imprisoned
