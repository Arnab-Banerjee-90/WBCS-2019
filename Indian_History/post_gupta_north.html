Post Gupta North India

The Maukharis
  • Held the region of western Uttar Pradesh
  • Isanavarman and Sarvavarman were powerful rulers
  • They adopted Rajadhiraja title
  • Succeeded by Avantivarman
  • Succeeded by Grahavarman
  • He married Rajyasri, the daughter of Pushyabhuti ruler Pravakarbardhan

The Maitrakas
  • Established capital in Saurashtra
  • Vallabhi was their capital
  • Dhruvasena II was the most important ruler

The Pushyabhutis
  • Pravakarvardhan (580 - 605 A.D) was the first important ruler he was succeeded by Rajyavardhan
  • Grahavarman was murdered by Devagupta in alliance with Shashanka
  • Rajyavardhan killed Devagupta, but was himself killed by Shashanka

Harshabardhan
  • Known as Siladitya.Harsha era started from 606 A.D
  • Kannauj was his capital
  • Last great Hindu ruler of India
  • Defeated Dhruvasena II, the Maitraka ruler of Vallabhi
  • Faced great resistance in the east by Shashanka
  • Defeated by Pulakeshi II, the Chalukya king of Batapi
  • He is mentioned as Sakaluttarapathanath in Aihole inscription
  • Chinese pilgrim Hiuen Tsang visited during his reign
  • Banbhatta (writer of Harshacharit and Kadambari) was his court poet
  • Harshavardhan himself wrote Nagananda,Ratnabali and Priyadarshika
  • He was a Shaivite
  • Used to celebrate a solemn festival at Prayag every five years

The Gaudas
  • Ruled over a territory in Bengal
  • Shashanka was the gratest ruler
  • Some believe he was a feudal lord under gupta leader Mahasengupta
  • He established the Gaur empire before 606
  • His capital was at Karnasubarna (near Baharampur)
  • He conquered Dandabhukti,Utkal and Kangod
  • He married Rajyashree the dauthger of Pravakarvardhan
  • He killed Rajyabardhan, the son of Pravakarvardhan
  • Harshavardhan was not able to harm Shashanka in any way
  • His empire paved the way for the future Pal and Sen rulers of Bengal
  • Died in 637

The Hunas
  • Barbarous race from central Asia
  • Established supremacy in North India
  • Toramana was the oldest ruler
  • Mihirkula was the most uncultered ruler
